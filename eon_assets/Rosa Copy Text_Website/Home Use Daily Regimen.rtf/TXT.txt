HOME USE- DAILY REGIMEN


1- Cleanse skin with gentle cleanser.
2- Tone. After toning splash water in your face and pat dry with a towel. .
3- Apply one pump of ReLuma to entire face, paying special attention to problem areas around eyes, mouth and forehead.
4- Apply moisturizer.
5- Apply sunblock of SPF 15 or higher.
